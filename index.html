1st AI Workshop on Research, Trends and Practices

Where: Bangalore | When: 20-21 February 2019

About

We are is putting together the AI Workshop on Research, Trends and Practices which is aimed at nurturing the academic and industry syndicate where accelerated computing is the common fabric. This workshop will bring select researchers/data scientists from key consumer internet companies of India where explosive growth of diverse applications of ML in production, the continued growth in data volume, and the complexity of large-scale learning systems are emerging.

The goal of this workshop is to bring together experts working at the crossroads of machine learning and software engineering to learn about SOTA & other engineering techniques.

Via these workshops, we wish to cover state of the art ML and AI platforms and algorithm toolkits, as well as dive into machine learning-focused developments in distributed learning platforms, programming languages, data structures, GPU processing, and other topics. Our plan is to mold this workshop to run annually (or bi-annually even!) to help build a strong community. We hope to include CFPs in future.

Key Topics

  • Deep Learning
  • Computer Vision
  • Generative Models
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • ML Systems Engineering and Platforms
